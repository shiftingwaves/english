### **Chapter 1: Meeting New Friends**  

**Mariam:** Assalaamu Alaikum!  
(Salaam, mai Mariam hoon.)  

**Sara:** Wa Alaikum Assalaam!  
(Wa Alaikum Salaam, mai Sara hoon.)  

**Mariam:** I am Mariam.  
(Mai Mariam hoon.)  

**Sara:** I am Sara.  
(Mai Sara hoon.)  

**Mariam:** How are you?  
(Aap kaise hain?)  

**Sara:** I am fine. Thank you!  
(Mai theek hoon, shukriya!)
